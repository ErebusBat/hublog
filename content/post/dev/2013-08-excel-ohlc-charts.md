HOWTO: Format Data for OHLC charts
==================================

Format data in this manner:

|  Date  |   Open  |  High  |  Low  |  Close  |
| ------ | ------- | ------ | ----- | ------- |
| 1/1/12 | 4.72202 | 5.4999 | 4.615 | 5.26766 |
| 1/2/12 | 5.26766 |   5.47 |   4.8 | 5.21678 |
